    Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients

    • 200g spaghetti
    • 100g pancetta
    • 2 eggs
    • 50g parmesan
    • Salt & pepper

    Instructions

    1. Cook spaghetti in salted water.
    2. Fry pancetta until crisp.
    3. Mix eggs and parmesan in a bowl.
    4. Drain pasta, remove from heat, mix with pancetta and egg-cheese mixture.
    5. Serve immediately with extra parmesan.

    Lasagna

    Ingredients

    • Lasagna sheets
    • 500g minced beef
    • 1 onion
    • 2 cups tomato sauce
    • 200g mozzarella
    • Bechamel sauce

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
    2. Cook minced beef with chopped onion until browned.
    3. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, bechamel, and mozzarella in baking dish.
    4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
    5. Bake 40-45 min until golden and bubbly.

    Tiramisu

    Ingredients

    • Ladyfingers
    • 250g mascarpone
    • 3 eggs
    • 100g sugar
    • Coffee
    • Cocoa powder

    Instructions

    1. Brew strong coffee and let it cool.
    2. Beat egg yolks with sugar, mix in mascarpone.
    3. Whip egg whites until stiff, fold into mascarpone mixture.
    4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer in dish.
    5. Spread cream mixture over layers, repeat.
    6. Dust with cocoa powder and chill 3-4 hours.
